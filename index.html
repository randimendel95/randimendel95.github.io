Research Grants in Australia

This interaction gives the reader the freedom to explore research grants in Australia, and compare aspects of those grants by sector of research.

How to manipulate the data

  • Click any of the 3 buttons to move the x-axis of the scatter plot to that aspect of the project
  • Select a factor to filter to only that type
  • Brush a region on the scatter plot to zoom into that region, and bar chart will only represent zoomed region
  • Hover over scatter plot or bar chart for more detail
